About Me

As of 2022 I have been a graduate from the University of Melbourne for a year where I studied for a Bachelor of Science and majored in Computing and Software Systems. When I was 11 my first I began programming using drag and drop in Game Maker to make a platformer games. In year 10 I picked up Python as a hobby and this then allowed me to get my first formal job at Codecamp where I would teach kids how to make their own games. This passion led me to swapping my major to Computing and Software Systems from Pharmacology. I now work as an Application Developer and Systems Support Engineer at a telecommunications company. This mainly involves working with PHP, MySQL and Wordpress, as well as the occasional bash script. On top of my full time work I am also a programming tutor. Outside of programming I enjoy playing chess, reading, walking and 3D printing.
