About Me

As of 2021 I am in my third year at the University of Melbourne where I have been studying a Bachelor of Science now majoring in Computing and Software Systems. When I was 11 my first programming was using drag and drop in Game Maker to make a platformer called "Chimpy and Baboony". In year 10 I picked up Python as a hobby and this then allowed me to get my first formal job at Codecamp where I would teach kids how to make their own games. This passion led me to swapping my major to Computing and Software Systems from Pharmacology. I am now teaching myself the MERN stack as I have found I enjoy developing on the full stack. On top of programming I enjoy playing chess, reading, walking and 3D printing.
